Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Introduction

Palette ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our color palette generation application.

Information We Collect

When you choose to create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • Display name (optional, for personalization)
  • Authentication provider (Apple or Google sign-in)
  • Account creation date

This information is used solely to provide account functionality and sync your data across devices.

Information We DON'T Collect

Palette is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do NOT track your usage or analytics
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
  • We do NOT access your photo library beyond color extraction

Local Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device:

  • Color palettes you save
  • Your favorite palettes
  • App preferences and settings
  • Palette generation history

If you sign in with an account, your palettes may be synced to provide backup and cross-device access.

Photos Access

If you choose to use the color extraction feature, the App will request access to your photo library. This access is used solely to:

  • Allow you to select photos for color extraction
  • Extract dominant colors from the selected photo

Photos are processed locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos anywhere.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: To securely manage user accounts and sign-in (Google, Apple Sign-In)
  • Apple StoreKit: To process in-app purchases and subscriptions

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Palette is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local data is protected by iOS encryption and your device passcode
  • Account authentication uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0
  • We use HTTPS for all network communications
  • Payment processing is handled securely by Apple

We recommend keeping your device secure with a strong passcode.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete all saved palettes at any time
  • You can clear your history from the Profile tab
  • You can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile tab
  • You can delete the App to remove all local data
  • You can revoke photo library access in iOS Settings

Account deletion permanently removes all your data from our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's settings page.

We're committed to protecting your privacy and will be happy to address any concerns you may have.
